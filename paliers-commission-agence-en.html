Agency commission — 7.5%

Single flat rate · % of compensation collected

The agency commission is a flat 7.5% of the CE 261 compensation actually collected, per successful passenger — the same rate for every partner, with no date condition.

Compensation collectedAgency commission (7.5%)Notes
€250≈ €18.75 (1,575 GMD)CE 261 — short-haul
€400≈ €30 (2,520 GMD)CE 261 — medium-haul
€600≈ €45 (3,800 GMD)CE 261 — long-haul (ref.)
The commission is always 7.5% of the amount actually obtained: if the airline pays less, the commission drops proportionally. GMD figures are indicative, at ≈ 84 GMD/€.

Paid within 48 business hours after Robin des Airs receives cleared compensation (Wave, Afrimoney, QMoney). EU 261 brackets (€250 / €400 / €600) are separate — see indemnity brackets.
