⚖️ Terms of the agreement
Article 1 — Purpose The Partner refers passengers who may be entitled to compensation under Regulation (EC) No 261/2004. Robin des Airs provides claim handling, airline correspondence, and recovery on a no win, no fee basis for the passenger. The Partner uses the certified partner portal (espace-agence) to submit cases and track status.
Article 2 — Agency commission Upon successful recovery and actual payment of compensation to the passenger, Robin des Airs pays the Partner 3,800 GMD per winning passenger (indicative ≈ €45 at 84 GMD/€ unless otherwise agreed in writing). Payment is made within 48 business hours via the method selected above, subject to valid payout details. No commission is due if the case fails or no compensation is collected.
Article 3 — Client files & technical email For each case, Robin des Airs creates a dedicated technical email address (lowercase case reference + @robindesairs.eu) as the file reference address for airline and third-party correspondence. Robin des Airs retains sole administration of that address. The Partner submits accurate passenger and flight data; Robin issues the passenger mandate link for signature. The Partner shall not collect a parallel fee from the passenger for the same EU 261 claim without prior written agreement.
Article 4 — Partner obligations The Partner shall: (i) provide accurate booking and contact data; (ii) obtain the passenger’s consent to be referred to Robin des Airs; (iii) not negotiate directly with the airline on the same incident without informing Robin des Airs; (iv) forward any airline contact to Robin within 24 hours; (v) use only the official portal or channels agreed with Robin.
Article 5 — Robin des Airs obligations Robin des Airs shall process referred cases diligently, keep the Partner informed of material status changes, maintain file traceability (emails, documents), and pay agreed commissions when due. Operational updates to the Partner may be sent via WhatsApp or other agreed channels, without exclusive reliance on a single channel for critical notices.
Article 6 — Term & termination This agreement runs for 24 months from signature and renews tacitly unless either party gives 30 days’ written notice. Either party may terminate immediately for material breach, fraud, or persistent non-cooperation.
Article 7 — Confidentiality & GDPR Each party shall protect personal data and use it only for claim management. Passengers are informed via the mandate and privacy policy. Data subject requests: partners@robindesairs.eu.
Article 8 — Non-circumvention During the term and for 12 months after, the Partner shall not bypass Robin des Airs on referred cases to avoid commission, including direct settlement with the airline on the same incident without Robin’s prior written consent.
Article 9 — Independent parties The Partner acts as an independent business. Nothing herein creates an employment, franchise, or joint venture relationship.
Article 10 — Governing law This agreement is governed by French law. Disputes shall first be addressed amicably; failing agreement, competent courts in France.
