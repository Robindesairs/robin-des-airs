Article 1 — Purpose

This Agreement establishes a referral partnership between Party A and Party B. Party B refers clients who may be eligible for flight delay or cancellation compensation under EU Regulation CE 261/2004. Party A processes the claims, engages with airlines, and manages the legal and administrative process. Party B earns a referral commission on each successfully recovered case.

Article 2 — Exclusive Territory

Party B is granted exclusive rights to refer cases originating from The Gambia, specifically covering:

  • Banjul International Airport (BJL) — all departures and arrivals
  • The Gambia — all passengers resident in or transiting through The Gambia
  • All eligible airlines operating routes from/to Gambia including Brussels Airlines, TUI Airways, TAP Air Portugal, and any other European carrier

During the term of this Agreement, Party A will not engage any other referral partner operating from the same territory. If a client from The Gambia contacts Robin des Airs independently (online or by phone), and requests local support, they will be directed to Seyman Travel as the designated local partner.

Article 3 — Commission

3,800 GMD per passenger on each successfully recovered compensation case.

  • Commission is due only upon effective collection of compensation from the airline
  • Commission is calculated per passenger named in the mandate, not per claim
  • If compensation is partially recovered, commission is adjusted pro-rata
  • Payment is transferred within 10 business days of Robin des Airs receiving the airline payment
  • No commission is due if the claim is lost — no risk to Party B

Article 4 — Obligations of Party B (Seyman Travel)

  • Inform eligible clients and refer them to Robin des Airs via the agency portal or WhatsApp
  • Assist in collecting basic documents (boarding pass, e-ticket, passport copy) when requested
  • Submit a minimum of 1 case per quarter to maintain exclusivity rights
  • Not submit claims on behalf of clients to any other flight compensation company during the term of this Agreement

Article 5 — Obligations of Party A (Robin des Airs)

  • Process all referred cases within 5 business days of receiving a complete mandate
  • Provide Party B with access to the online partner portal showing real-time case status
  • Handle all legal, administrative, and airline negotiation processes independently
  • Notify Party B promptly when compensation is received
  • Maintain the confidentiality of all client data in accordance with GDPR

Article 6 — Non-Compete & Domain Protection

This clause protects both parties' core business from mutual competition.

6.1Party A undertakes not to:

  • Sell or book airline tickets, hotel stays, or any travel products
  • Organise tours, excursions, or travel packages
  • Provide visa services, travel insurance, or currency exchange
  • Conduct any activity that constitutes a travel agency service
  • Directly solicit Seyman Travel's existing client base for services outside the scope of flight compensation

6.2Party B undertakes not to:

  • Process, manage, or pursue flight compensation claims independently or in partnership with any other claims company
  • Hold itself out as a flight compensation specialist or claims management company
  • Charge clients any fees related to flight compensation processing
  • Use Robin des Airs' processes, templates, or methodologies for independent commercial purposes

6.3Duration of non-compete obligations:

The obligations in Articles 6.1 and 6.2 apply during the entire term of this Agreement and for a period of 6 months following its termination, regardless of the reason for termination.

Article 7 — Term, Renewal & Termination

7.1Initial term: This Agreement enters into force upon signature and runs for a period of one quarter (3 months).

7.2Automatic renewal: At the end of each quarter, this Agreement is automatically renewed for an additional quarter unless either party provides written notice of termination at least 30 days before the end of the current quarter.

7.3Termination for cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately and without notice in case of material breach, including but not limited to: non-payment of commissions, breach of the non-compete clause, or submission of fraudulent claims.

7.4Effect of termination: Commissions accrued before the termination date remain payable. Cases already submitted but not yet resolved at the time of termination will continue to be processed and commissions paid normally.

Article 8 — Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding the commercial terms of this Agreement, client data, and any proprietary methods, systems, or processes used by either party. This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement for a period of 2 years.

Article 9 — Liability

Robin des Airs acts as the sole legal representative in all compensation claims. Party B bears no legal liability for the outcome of any claim. Robin des Airs' liability to Party B is limited to the unpaid commissions due at the time of any dispute.

Article 10 — Governing Law & Disputes

This Agreement is governed by French law. In the event of a dispute, the parties agree to seek an amicable resolution within 30 days. Failing that, the dispute shall be submitted to the competent courts of Paris, France.

Article 11 — Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
