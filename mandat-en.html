Article 1, Purpose, assignment regime and case email address
💡 In plain terms: You entrust Robin des Airs with recovering the money the airline owes you, by assigning it your claim so that it collects it in your place. You remain the beneficiary (you receive 75%) and you keep your consumer status, hence access to mediation. Connecting flight? It is the final arrival that counts, and it is covered.
This instrument is an ASSIGNMENT-OF-CLAIM AGREEMENT granted FOR COLLECTION PURPOSES ONLY (Articles 1321 et seq. of the French Civil Code). The Assignor (the passenger) assigns to Robin des Airs (the Assignee), for collection purposes, its born or future claim under Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 fixed compensation (art. 7), reimbursements and care (arts. 8 and 9) and, where applicable, further damage (art. 12) arising from the delay, cancellation or denied boarding that is the subject of this instrument (the « Incident »).

Assignment for collection purposes (and not a full outright sale-assignment). Robin des Airs collects as assignee and pays the Assignor its net share (75%) under the terms of Article 5. The claim is neither acquired definitively for value, nor resold: the economic value recovered remains allocated to the Assignor, subject only to deduction of the fee (Article 4).

Deferred enforceability, preservation of mediation. Between the parties, the assignment takes effect upon signature (art. 1321 French Civil Code). However, it is NOT notified to the carrier for as long as the amicable phase and consumer mediation have not been conducted: during that phase, Robin des Airs acts ON BEHALF OF THE ASSIGNOR, who EXPRESSLY RETAINS CONSUMER STATUS and access to consumer mediation (arts. L.611-1 et seq. of the French Consumer Code). The assignment is made enforceable against the carrier (assigned debtor) only by its notification (art. 1324 French Civil Code), which occurs only at the litigation stage, after failure of mediation (Article 5 bis).

Connections. Where the journey includes one or more connections under a single booking, the assignment covers the entire journey : compensation is determined by the final destination and the total distance, the delay being assessed at arrival at the final destination (CJEU, 26 February 2013, Folkerts, C-11/11).

Case email address. Robin des Airs creates a dedicated technical email address (case reference followed by « @robindesairs.eu » , e.g. : ref@robindesairs.eu) constituting the reference address for all exchanges with the airline, its representatives and any third party ; Robin des Airs alone administers it. The Assignor expressly authorises its creation and use for the term of the agreement, including for formal notices and procedural acts, and keeps access, copies and documents in order to ensure traceability of the file. Operational updates to the Assignor may be provided via WhatsApp or any other agreed channel, without being exclusive.

Language. This agreement is a bilingual instrument drawn up in French and English, the French version being the only authoritative one. The Assignor authorises Robin des Airs to establish, produce and rely on the English version (or a faithful translation) before the airline and any third party. In the event of any discrepancy, the signed French original prevails. The signature covers the instrument in both languages.
Article 1 bis, Recovery phases and notification of the assignment
💡 In plain terms: As long as we settle amicably and in mediation, we act for you, without disclosing the assignment to the airline: you remain a consumer. If we have to go to court, we then notify the assignment and it is always a lawyer who pleads, Robin des Airs never pleads alone.
The assignment (Article 1) is granted upon signature between the parties, but its enforceability against the carrier is organised in two stages, in the sole interest of the Assignor :

(1) Amicable phase and mediation, assignment NOT notified. Robin des Airs presents, follows up and pursues the claim ON BEHALF OF THE ASSIGNOR with the airline and then, where applicable, with the competent consumer mediation body (mandatory prior mediation, Decree No 2025-772 of 5 August 2025). The Assignor retains consumer status and access to mediation ; the assignment is not notified to the carrier during this phase.

(2) Litigation phase, notification of the assignment. If mediation fails, Robin des Airs notifies the assignment to the carrier (art. 1324 French Civil Code) ; from that notification, only payment made into the hands of Robin des Airs is discharging, and Robin des Airs acts in its own name, as assignee, for contentious recovery.

Procedural discipline. The regime enforceable against the carrier is fixed and notified before the relevant act ; Robin des Airs invokes only one regime per instance in order to preserve its standing to act.

Representation in court by a lawyer. Robin des Airs carries out no activity reserved to lawyers and never appears alone before a court. Any judicial proceedings are necessarily conducted by a lawyer registered with a bar, appointed for that purpose, who alone ensures representation and pleading. The sole purpose of the assignment is to secure recovery and in no way to allow Robin des Airs to plead in person.

This arrangement changes neither the fee (Article 4), nor the withdrawal right (Article 9), nor the obligation to pay the Assignor its net share (75%).
Article 2, Term
💡 In plain terms: The agreement lasts 24 months. It can be ended by either side in the event of a serious problem (15 days' notice). And if the amicable steps do not succeed, you may freely terminate it.
This agreement is granted for 24 months from signature. This term is necessary to handle complex cases involving follow-ups, mediation and court proceedings if needed. It may be terminated by email at any time in the event of serious breach, established fraud or persistent non-cooperation by one of the parties, with 15 days' notice. In addition, where the amicable phase has been exhausted without result and no court proceedings have been brought, the Assignor may freely terminate (for convenience) by email, with 15 days' notice, free of charge ; the claim is then automatically re-assigned to it (Article 5 bis).
Article 3, Exclusivity & anti-circumvention
💡 In plain terms: In the meantime, do not entrust the same case to anyone else and do not negotiate alone with the airline without telling us.
This agreement is exclusive. As the claim is assigned to Robin des Airs (Article 1), the Assignor undertakes, for the term of the agreement, not to assign or entrust the same case to a third party, nor to negotiate directly with the airline for the same Incident without first informing Robin des Airs. Only an agreement entered into in bad faith, for the sole purpose of evading the fee due to Robin des Airs, engages the Assignor's liability and leaves it liable for that fee ; a good-faith step by the Assignor entails no penalty.
Article 4, Fees · No Win, No Fee

Si nous ne récupérons rien, vous ne payez rien et la créance vous revient. Avant tout dépôt au tribunal, on vous prévient par WhatsApp et email.
La cession étant consentie à titre de recouvrement, la rémunération de Robin des Airs est fixée comme suit, uniquement en cas de succès :

(i) Phase amiable et négociation pré-contentieuse (réclamation directe à la compagnie aérienne, mise en demeure officielle par l'avocat partenaire, négociation avec les conseils de la compagnie) :
  • Robin des Airs conserve 25 % TTC du montant effectivement encaissé.

  • Robin des Airs conserve 45 % TTC du montant effectivement encaissé. Cette majoration couvre intégralement les frais d'avocat partenaire, d'huissier, de greffe et de plateforme judiciaire, tous avancés par Robin des Airs.
  • The Assignor (you) perçoit 55 % nets versés sous 48 h après encaissement effectif.
  • Exemple : sur 600 € recouvrés, 270 € reviennent à Robin des Airs et 330 € vous sont versés.


Information préalable au passage en contentieux : le Cédant reconnaît avoir pris connaissance de la double tarification (25 % / 45 %) avant la signature des présentes et consent à l'application automatique du taux de 45 % en cas de passage en phase contentieuse, sans qu'un nouvel accord ne soit requis. Robin des Airs s'engage néanmoins à informer le Cédant par WhatsApp et/ou email avant tout dépôt d'assignation. Le Cédant conserve à tout moment la faculté de mettre fin au dossier dans les conditions de l'article 9 (droit de rétractation) ou de l'article 2 (résiliation amiable après épuisement de la phase amiable).

Ces taux s'appliquent à l'ensemble des sommes recouvrées au titre de l'indemnité du Cédant (indemnité forfaitaire art. 7, remboursements et prise en charge art. 8 et 9, préjudice complémentaire art. 12). L'article 700 du Code de procédure civile, les dépens et les intérêts moratoires alloués par le juge sont conservés en intégralité par Robin des Airs et son avocat partenaire selon convention, et ne sont pas inclus dans la base de calcul du Cédant. En cas d'échec ou de non-recouvrement, aucune somme n'est due par le Cédant, aucune avance n'est exigée à aucun stade, et la créance lui est rétrocédée de plein droit (« No Win No Fee »).

The split also applies to any compensation in kind (voucher, credit note, miles…) accepted for the case, calculated on its value ; the Assignor undertakes not to accept such compensation without the agreement of Robin des Airs. The Assignor requires payment in cash (bank transfer) and expressly refuses, as of now, any voucher, credit note, miles or other service offered in lieu thereof : under Article 7(3) of Regulation (EC) No 261/2004, compensation in kind requires the passenger's signed agreement, which the Assignor does not give.

Subject of the assignment. The Assignor assigns to Robin des Airs, for collection purposes, the born or future compensation claim it holds against the operating air carrier that performed the flight (art. 2(b) of the Regulation), regardless of the commercial flight number or marketing carrier (codeshare), under Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 for the Incident, including the fixed compensation (art. 7), reimbursements and expenses (arts. 8 and 9), further damage (art. 12), interest, penalties and ancillary amounts.

Effect between the parties. Under Articles 1321 to 1326 of the French Civil Code, the assignment takes effect between the parties upon signature and covers a determinable claim (parties, flight, date, EU 261/2004 basis).

Continuity clause. Article 5 ter, Unenforceability of non-assignment clauses
💡 In plain terms: If the airline claims you were not allowed to assign the case to us, that is false: the European courts have settled it.
The Assignor declares awareness of the airline's general conditions of carriage. Any clause of the general conditions of carriage prohibiting or restricting the assignment of the compensation claim due under Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 is unenforceable : the Court of Justice of the European Union has held that such a clause is contrary to Article 15 of the Regulation (CJEU, 6 February 2025, Eventmedia Soluciones v Air Europa Líneas Aéreas, C-11/23). Accordingly, the airline may not rely on such a clause against the assignment granted to Robin des Airs (Articles 1 and 5 bis).

Article 11, Loi applicable, juridictions & litiges
💡 In plain terms: Droit français. Si on doit aller au tribunal contre la compagnie, c'est le tribunal de commerce de Paris (procédure rapide). Si vous avez un litige avec nous, c'est le tribunal de votre lieu de résidence (protection consommateur).
Le présent contrat est soumis au droit français. En cas de litige, les parties privilégient une résolution amiable dans les 30 jours.

Élection de juridiction :
  • Litiges entre Robin des Airs et le transporteur aérien (recouvrement de la créance cédée) : compétence exclusive du Tribunal de commerce de Paris, en application des articles L. 721-3 et L. 721-5 du Code de commerce (cession entre commerçants). Cette juridiction est retenue pour sa rapidité et sa spécialisation, dans l'intérêt commun des parties.
  • Litiges entre Robin des Airs et le Cédant (consommateur) : compétence du tribunal du lieu de résidence du Cédant ou du lieu de l'exécution du contrat, conformément à l'article R. 631-3 du Code de la consommation, sans qu'aucune clause attributive de compétence ne puisse y déroger.
Médiation de la consommation préalable : avant tout recours juridictionnel entre Robin des Airs et le Cédant, ce dernier peut saisir gratuitement la Médiation du Tourisme et du Voyage (MTV), médiateur désigné par le Prestataire (cf. CGV article 13).

Divisibilité : si une stipulation du présent contrat est jugée nulle ou inopposable, les autres conservent leur plein effet, la stipulation concernée étant réputée non écrite ou réduite dans la mesure permise par la loi.

Survie : la fin du contrat ne met pas fin aux stipulations which, by their nature, survive, in particular confidentiality and personal data, the obligation to remit the commission in the event of a later payment in connection with the incident, intellectual property, liability and dispute resolution.
