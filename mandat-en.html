⚖️ Mandate terms
Article 1 — Purpose and case email address The Client grants Robin des Airs an exclusive mandate to obtain compensation under Regulation (EC) No 261/2004, by amicable, administrative, judicial or arbitral means.

Robin des Airs creates a unique technical email address: lowercase mandate reference + @robindesairs.eu (ex. : ref@robindesairs.eu). This is the reference address for the file for exchanges with the airline and any third party involved in recovery. Robin des Airs alone administers and manages it.

The Client expressly authorises Robin des Airs to create and use this address for the mandate term, including formal notices and procedural steps. Robin des Airs keeps access, copies and documents for file traceability.

The Client acknowledges Robin des Airs may use the Client's personal email if required, as stated in this mandate or later.

Operational updates to the Client may use WhatsApp or any other agreed channel, without these channels being exclusive for important notices.
Article 2 — Term The mandate runs 24 months from signature. It may be terminated by email for serious breach, fraud or persistent non-cooperation, with 15 days' notice.
Article 3 — Exclusivity & anti-circumvention This mandate is exclusive. The Client shall not assign the same case elsewhere nor negotiate with the airline for the same incident without prior notice to Robin des Airs.
Article 4 — Fees · No win, no fee Robin des Airs charges 25% incl. VAT of amounts collected, success fee only. If the case fails, no fee is due. No upfront payment.
Article 4.1 — Legal costs Robin des Airs covers 100% of legal and court costs. The Client pays nothing upfront. Robin des Airs may select viable cases before court action.

Article 5 — Payment & client protection The Client instructs the airline to pay to the account designated by Robin des Airs. Within 48 business hours, Robin pays the Client's net share (75%). Direct payment to the Client requires immediate remittance of the 25% fee.
Article 6 — Non-release clause (airline) Payment by the airline directly to the Client does not release the airline towards Robin des Airs as exclusive representative. Robin des Airs notifie expressément la compagnie, dès la mise en demeure, que le seul paiement libératoire valable est celui effectué sur le compte bancaire de Robin des Airs.

Accordingly: (i) direct payment to the Client in breach of this instruction is not release towards Robin des Airs; (ii) the airline remains liable to the representative; (iii) Robin des Airs may pursue recovery including court action.
Article 7 — Robin des Airs obligations Robin des Airs shall: (i) process diligently; (ii) inform at key stages; (iii) pay within 48 business hours after collection; (iv) notify the airline of this mandate; (v) protect data under GDPR.
Article 8 — Client obligations The Client shall: (i) provide accurate information; (ii) send documents promptly; (iii) notify Robin des Airs within 24h of airline contact; (iv) not accept airline payment without notice; (v) not act in parallel without written consent.
Article 9 — 14-day withdrawal & immediate start Under French consumer law, the Client has 14 calendar days to withdraw by email to contact@robindesairs.eu with « I withdraw — Ref. [case number] ».

If the Client requests immediate performance before 14 days end (box below), Robin des Airs may start without waiting. Withdrawal within that period may make completed services due pro rata — no amount before compensation is collected.
Article 10 — Confidentiality & GDPR Personal data is used only for case management, not sold. GDPR rights: contact@robindesairs.eu.
Article 11 — Governing law & disputes This mandate is governed by French law. Disputes shall be resolved amicably within 30 days; failing agreement, competent courts apply.
