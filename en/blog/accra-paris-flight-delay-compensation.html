Accra Kotoka International (ACC) is a major Ghana hub with direct flights to Paris and connecting flights via European hubs. Direct Accra-Paris ≈4,800 km, qualifying for €600 EC 261 compensation if operated by an EU airline.

Accra-Paris distance and compensation

  • Distance: ≈4,800 km direct
  • EC 261 bracket: >3,500 km
  • Compensation: €600 per passenger if delayed 3h+ at Paris
  • Family of 4: €2,400 total

Common delays on Accra routes

  • Harmattan visibility (Dec-Feb): predictable, generally not extraordinary unless exceptional.
  • Accra airport congestion: standard scheduling issue.
  • Connection at European hub: late inbound rotation = internal scheduling risk, not extraordinary.
  • Crew availability: not extraordinary (Krüsemann ruling).

Brussels Airlines: the main EU operator on Ghana

Brussels Airlines is a major Ghana-EU connector (Lufthansa Group). It serves Accra daily from Brussels. Many Ghana-Paris bookings transit via Brussels. EC 261 covers the entire journey on a single ticket.

Common Brussels Airlines refusal: "Connection delay due to inbound aircraft technical issue". Per Wallentin-Hermann, technical issues are NOT extraordinary. The refusal is contestable.

How to claim from the Ghana diaspora

  1. Keep evidence: boarding pass, PNR, photo of arrival time at Paris (CDG departure board or your phone).
  2. Submit online claim on the operating airline's website (Air France EN, Brussels Airlines EN, Lufthansa EN).
  3. Provide IBAN: an EU bank account works fastest. A Ghana account works but takes longer.
  4. Wait 30-60 days for response. Expect a partial offer or refusal first.
  5. Escalate to French DGAC or Belgian DGTA if refused.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Accra-Paris route covered by EC 261?
Yes if operated by an EU airline: Air France direct, Lufthansa via Frankfurt, Brussels Airlines via Brussels, KLM via Amsterdam, Royal Air Maroc via Casablanca. For non-EU airlines, EC 261 applies only on EU-departing segments.
Distance and compensation Accra-Paris?
Accra-Paris direct ≈4,800 km. EC 261 compensation: €600 per passenger if delayed 3h+ at Paris. Family of 4 = €2,400 total.
Brussels Airlines Accra-Paris via Brussels — same compensation?
Yes, on a single ticket Accra-Brussels-Paris. EC 261 covers the whole journey on an EU operator. Total Accra-Paris distance counts (~4,800 km) = €600 if final delay 3h+.
What if my flight is on Africa World Airlines or Ghana airlines (non-EU)?
For Accra → EU on a non-EU carrier, EC 261 does NOT apply. Ghanaian aviation rules and bilateral agreements offer limited protection. The Montreal Convention applies for baggage. Take EU airline alternatives for EC 261 coverage.
How to claim from Ghana diaspora?
Submit online form on the operating airline's website (English available). Provide PNR, boarding pass, IBAN (EU or Ghana account). Claim handled by airline's European operations. Escalate to DGAC (France) or LBA (Germany) if refused.
