Royal Air Maroc (RAM) is Morocco's flag carrier and a major airline on Europe-Africa routes. It operates a Casablanca hub connecting Europe to West Africa, the Maghreb and increasingly the Americas. EC 261 applies for EU-departing flights.

When RAM is covered by EC 261

Morocco is NOT in the EU. EC 261 applies only:

  • If the flight departs from an EU airport: Paris-Casablanca, Brussels-Casablanca, etc.
  • If the single ticket starts in the EU and crosses Casablanca to a third destination (e.g. Paris-Casablanca-Dakar)

It does NOT apply:

  • Casablanca → EU on RAM (non-EU departure)
  • Casablanca → Dakar on RAM (no EU connection)
  • Tickets bought as separate segments where only the non-EU leg is delayed

RAM Europe-Africa connections via Casablanca

Single tickets via Casablanca to onward destinations: this is where RAM is competitive. EC 261 applies on the total distance:

  • Paris → Casablanca → Dakar: ≈4,200 km = €600 if delayed at Dakar 3h+
  • Paris → Casablanca → Abidjan: ≈4,500 km = €600
  • Brussels → Casablanca → Conakry: ≈4,500 km = €600
  • Paris → Casablanca → Bamako: ≈4,000 km = €600
  • Paris → Casablanca → Cotonou: ≈4,800 km = €600

RAM's typical defenses

  • "Crew availability" — internal staff management, not extraordinary (Krüsemann ruling, 2018). Defeated.
  • "Aircraft rotation delay from previous flight" — scheduling risk, not extraordinary (Sturgeon, 2009). Defeated.
  • "Sandstorm in Marrakech / weather" — only valid if NOTAM-confirmed extraordinary event. Predictable sand winds in spring/summer = not extraordinary.
  • "Technical issue" — Wallentin-Hermann: not extraordinary. Defeated.

Claim process against RAM

  1. Online form: royalairmaroc.com → "Help" → Customer Relations
  2. Documents: PNR (Air Maroc reservation), boarding pass, ID, IBAN
  3. Wait 60-120 days: RAM is one of the slower airlines
  4. If refused: escalate to French DGAC or Belgian DGTA. RAM has substantial European assets (route slots) and complies with enforcement orders.
  5. Backup: court action in EU jurisdiction (often Brussels or Paris)

Special case: Tangier, Marrakech, Agadir, Fez direct flights

RAM operates seasonal direct flights to other Moroccan cities. Same EC 261 rules apply if departure is from EU:

  • Paris → Marrakech: ≈2,000 km = €400
  • Paris → Agadir: ≈2,200 km = €400
  • Brussels → Tangier: ≈2,000 km = €400

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Royal Air Maroc covered by EC 261?
Partially. Morocco is NOT in the EU. EC 261 applies if the flight DEPARTS from an EU airport (Paris-Casablanca, Brussels-Casablanca). It does NOT apply for Casablanca → EU flights on Royal Air Maroc (non-EU departure + non-EU airline).
Paris-Casablanca delayed — what compensation?
€400 per passenger if delayed 3h+ at Casablanca (distance ≈1,900 km, 1,500-3,500 km bracket). Plus right to care during the wait.
Casablanca-Dakar on Royal Air Maroc delayed — is EC 261 applicable?
No. Both airports are outside the EU and Royal Air Maroc is a Moroccan airline. EC 261 does not apply. Moroccan and international air carriage rules (Montreal Convention for baggage) apply but offer much less protection.
Paris-Casablanca-Dakar single ticket delayed at final destination — EC 261?
Yes. Single ticket from EU origin, EC 261 applies to the whole journey. If you arrive at Dakar 3h+ late, compensation is based on total Paris-Dakar distance (≈4,200 km = €600). Royal Air Maroc is the operating carrier and responsible.
How to claim against Royal Air Maroc?
Royalairmaroc.com customer relations, by email or via mobile app. Response time 60-120 days. RAM often refuses first claims — escalate to French DGAC or Belgian DGTA. RAM has European assets and complies with enforcement.
