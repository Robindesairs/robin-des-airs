FAQ

Everything you need to know

Answers agencies ask before joining.

Does my agency pay anything to join?
No. 3,800 GMD (tier at signing) is paid by Robin des Airs to your agency on successful claims. Your agency pays nothing. No financial risk if a claim fails.
When do I get paid?
Once the passenger has actually received airline compensation, Robin des Airs triggers your commission, typically within 48 business hours via agreed payout (bank, Wave, etc.).
Can my clients receive up to €600?
EC 261/2004 sets compensation by distance and incident type. The indicative cap on the longest segments is €600 (about 50,400 GMD at 84 GMD/€) per passenger. The amount your client receives depends on the airline and case.
How is commission calculated in The Gambia?
Commission is in Gambian dalasi (GMD) per compensated passenger, tier locked at signing. See commission tiers and sign the partner agreement. Founding partners (3 slots before 31 Aug 2026): 4,000 GMD/pax.
How do I send a case?
Once partnered, Robin des Airs monitors relevant situations and notifies you early no manual report for every delay. Share the flight when the client books. For onboarding or edge cases: partners@robindesairs.eu, the form below, or WhatsApp +33 6 77 47 01 22.
Are my travellers’ data protected?
Yes. Data is hosted in Europe with GDPR-compliant processing. Client confidentiality is a priority.
Which flights are eligible?
Flights under EC 261/2004: significant arrival delay, cancellation, denied boarding or connection impact when the itinerary remains covered, sub-Saharan Africa ↔ Europe on EU carriers or departing from a European airport.
Can I contact you on WhatsApp?
Yes. Message +33 6 77 47 01 22 (include your country code). Great for a first chat; day-to-day, early alerts on monitored files are the main channel.
